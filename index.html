DeadEndRPG

Dead End is a Tabletop Roleplaying Game that takes place in modern times, with modern technology and modern problems. Our team's goal is to create a more complex and punishing Tabletop RPG than the industry's standard; i.e., D&D5e and SW5e. However, we still strive to give you the same sense of immersion and swift gameplay you get with the aforementioned games.
